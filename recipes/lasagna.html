How to Make Homemade Lasagna

Description

A true classic, this lasagna recipe is a journey of rich, comforting flavors and textures that will transport you straight to the heart of Italy.

  • Aromatics
  • Tomatoes
  • Seasonings

Steps

  1. In a shallow dish, whisk together eggs, milk, sugar, vanilla extract, and cinnamon. Lay out eight thick slices of day-old bread, ready for dipping.
  2. Dip each slice of bread into the egg custard, allowing it to soak for about 20-30 seconds on each side until it is saturated but not falling apa
  3. Melt butter on a large skillet or griddle over medium heat. Place the soaked bread slices on the hot skillet and cook for 3-4 minutes per side, until each side is beautifully golden brown and the center is cooked through.
  4. Transfer the cooked French toast to plates. Serve hot with your favorite toppings, such as maple syrup, powdered sugar, and fresh berries.
