French Toast

Description

A timeless breakfast classic, this French toast recipe creates a dish that is wonderfully custardy on the inside with a crisp, golden-brown exterior. The secret is using thick-sliced, slightly stale bread which soaks up the rich egg mixture without becoming soggy. It's a simple yet elegant meal perfect for a lazy weekend brunch.

Ingredients

  • 8 slices thick-cut bread
  • 4 large eggs
  • A cup of milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 2 teaspoon granulated cinnamon
  • Pinch of salt
  • Butter
  • Marple syrup
