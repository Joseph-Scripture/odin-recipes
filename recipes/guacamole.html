Simple and Easy Guacamole

Description

This is the ultimate quick and easy guacamole recipe, focusing on fresh ingredients and authentic flavor. The key is to use ripe avocados and fresh lime juice. It a versatile dip that always a hit at parties and a fantastic topping for tacos, salads, and more.

  • 3 ripe avocados
  • 1 small red onion, finely chopped
  • A pinch of ground cumin
  • fresh cilantro
  • Juice of 1 lime
  • jalapeno
  • kosher salt
