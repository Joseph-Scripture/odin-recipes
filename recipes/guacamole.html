Simple and Easy Guacamole

Description

This is the ultimate quick and easy guacamole recipe, focusing on fresh ingredients and authentic flavor. The key is to use ripe avocados and fresh lime juice. It a versatile dip that always a hit at parties and a fantastic topping for tacos, salads, and more.

  • 3 ripe avocados
  • 1 small red onion, finely chopped
  • A pinch of ground cumin
  • fresh cilantro
  • Juice of 1 lime
  • jalapeno
  • kosher salt

Steps

  1. Prepare the Avocados: Slice the avocados in half, remove the pits, and scoop the flesh into a medium bowl.
  2. Mash: Using a fork or a potato masher, gently mash the avocado to your desired consistency. Some prefer it chunky, others smooth.
  3. Combine Ingredients: Add the chopped red onion, jalape√±o (if using), cilantro, lime juice, and salt to the mashed avocado.
  4. Mix and Season: Stir gently until all the ingredients are combined. Taste and adjust seasoning as needed with more salt or lime juice.
  5. Serve: Serve immediately with your favorite tortilla chips. To store, press plastic wrap directly onto the surface of the guacamole to prevent browning.
